Question: How do I join offline course?
Answer: You can join offline course by directly coming to the institute on the starting day of the course.

Question: How do I pay for offline course?
Answer: You can pay in cash at the institute or even make payment online using any of the options mentioned in Online Payments section.

Question: Can I use lab facility?
Answer: We do not provide lab facility. You have to practice on your own.

Question: Do I get any course material?
Answer: For some courses we provide printed course material and for others we provide digital course material.
